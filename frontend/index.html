🔍 Key Observations

⚖️ Severe Class Imbalance

99.83% normal vs 0.17% fraud — standard accuracy is misleading. Focus on Recall and F1.

💵 Amount Patterns

Fraud transactions (avg $122) trend slightly higher than normal (avg $88). Some outliers exist.

🔬 PCA Features

V1–V28 are anonymized via PCA for privacy. Some Vs show strong discriminating power.

🧬 SMOTE Strategy

Synthetic Minority Oversampling creates 226K fraud samples to balance against 226K normal.

💡 Why Random Forest?

Threshold 0.4

Lowering the decision threshold from 0.5 → 0.4 boosts recall from 76% → 83% at the cost of some precision.

SMOTE Training

Training on SMOTE-balanced data (not undersampled) gives best balance between precision and recall.

GridSearchCV

Best params: max_depth=10, n_estimators=100. Cross-validated recall: 98.2% on training folds.

ROC-AUC 0.99

Near-perfect area under curve means the model ranks frauds well above normal transactions.
